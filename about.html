App Information

About This App

Purpose

This app helps communities in Ghana monitor flood risk, report incidents, and visualize flood-prone hotspots for awareness and preparedness.

Created by "Nketia Derrick Franz, Addai Enoch Wiafe, and Vinod".

Data Sources

Forecast & Historical Weather

Open-Meteo APIs (forecast, archive, geocoding).

Maps & Geospatial Display

Leaflet + OpenStreetMap.

Coverage

Ghana-focused flood monitoring and reporting.

Disclaimer

This platform provides risk estimates and community reports. It is not an official emergency warning system. Always follow NADMO, Police, and local authorities for life-safety decisions.
