Help & Support

Quick Help

  • If current location fails, run app on `http://localhost` and allow location access.
  • If a place is not found, try adding district or nearby landmark.
  • Use Flood Photo Reports to submit visual flood evidence.

Safety by Risk Level

High Flood Risk

Move to higher ground, avoid driving, and call emergency numbers immediately when needed.

Moderate Flood Risk

Prepare evacuation items, monitor alerts, and avoid vulnerable routes.

Low Flood Risk

Continue routine monitoring and report blocked drains or rising water early.
